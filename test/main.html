Part b

Test cite [1] The complex spherical harmonics \({Y}_{\ell }^{m} : S^2 \to \mathbb {C}\) of degree \(\ell \) and order \(m\) are defined as: \begin {equation} {Y}_{\ell }^{m} (\theta ,\varphi ) = {(-1)}^{m} N_{\ell m} P_{\ell }^{m}(cos(\theta )) e^{i \ell \varphi } \end {equation}

Where \(P_{\ell }^{m}\) are the associated Legendre polynomials and \(N_{\ell m}\) is the normalization factor: \begin {equation} N_{\ell m} = \sqrt {\frac {2 \ell + 1}{4 \pi } \frac {(\ell - m)!}{(\ell + m)!}} \end {equation}

The real spherical harmonics \(\mathcal {Y}_{\ell }^{m} : S^2 \to \mathbb {R}\) can then be derived as: \begin {equation} \mathcal {Y}_{\ell }^{m} = \begin {cases} \sqrt {2}(-1)^m \operatorname {Im}\left [ {Y}_{\ell }^{\left | m \right |} \right ] & \text {if } m < 0\\ {Y}_{\ell }^{0} & \text {if } m = 0\\ \sqrt {2}(-1)^m \operatorname {Re}\left [ {Y}_{\ell }^{m} \right ] & \text {if } m > 0\\ \end {cases} \end {equation}
